About Us

Welcome to Sri Hari Women’s Hostel, a safe, budget-friendly, and thoughtfully managed accommodation for working women and students in Keeranatham, Coimbatore. Located just 500 meters from CHIL SEZ and a short walk from GR Bakery, our hostel is ideal for professionals working in IT parks and students attending nearby colleges like Shankara College of Science and Commerce.

Whether you're beginning your career or pursuing your degree, Sri Hari provides a peaceful, secure, and community-like environment that truly feels like home. Paying Guest accommodation is also available, making it flexible for various needs.
